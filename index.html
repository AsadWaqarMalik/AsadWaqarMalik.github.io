Biography

Asad W. Malik (M’19-SM’20) received the Ph.D. degree in computer software engineering from NUST, Pakistan. During his PhD he worked under the supervision of Prof. Richard Fujimoto at Georgia Institute of Technology, USA. He is currently an Associate Professor with the School of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science, NUST. Previously, he worked as a senior lecturer at the University of Malaya, Malaysia. He also served as a research scholar at Research scholar at Challey Institute, North Dakota State University, USA. His research interests include parallel and distributed simulation, cloud computing, and vehicular networks. He is the PI on the distributed simulation projects such as CloudNetSim, FogNetSim, and SEECSim.
